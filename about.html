Our Story

Haile Resort Arba Minch is part of the Haile Hotels & Resorts chain, founded by Ethiopian Olympic gold medalist Haile Gebrselassie. The resort opened in 2017, marking a significant addition to Ethiopia's hospitality sector.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every guest feels at home, offering personalized service and attention to detail in every aspect of your stay.
